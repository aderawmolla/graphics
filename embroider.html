This captivating design draws inspiration from the joyful weaving hands of a young Ethiopian girl. The ensemble features a light gray t-shirt paired with trousers, embodying subtle elegance and simplicity. Adorning the attire are intricate diamond patterns, each telling a unique story:

  • A delicate diamond on the shoulder adds a touch of charm and grace.
  • A similar diamond on the back subtly enhances the design, creating a cohesive look.
  • Small diamond accents on the legs bring an element of unity to the entire outfit.
